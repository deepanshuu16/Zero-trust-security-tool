Core Mission

A cybersecurity companion students can actually use.

This platform focuses on practical protection instead of enterprise-only jargon. It brings together scanning, awareness, and personal security checkups in one place.

What it solves

  • Fake internship and scholarship links
  • Weak or reused passwords
  • Scam messages and phishing attempts
  • Unsafe browsing habits and shared network risks

Why it feels better

  • Cleaner layout with accessible typography
  • Simple scores and plain-language advice
  • Practical tools instead of empty dashboards
  • Beginner-friendly learning built into the product
