Quick Lessons
  • Never share OTPs, recovery codes, or one-time sign-in links.
  • Fake websites often use extra letters, odd TLDs, or misleading subdomains.
  • Public Wi-Fi is safer when you avoid sensitive logins and stick to HTTPS websites.
